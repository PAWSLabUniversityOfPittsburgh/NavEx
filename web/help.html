Overview

NavEx is a tool to provide adaptive Navigation to Examples. NavEx window is subdivided into the following areas (see figure below) :
  • Knowledge map area (A) here you can monitor you current progress with dossections;
  • Dissection title are (B) tells you what is the name of the dissection and what lecture it belongs to;
  • Menu area (C) gives you control over the NavEx visualization;
  • Dissection area (D) disp;ays dissection itself.

Below some NavEx window areas are explained in more detail.

Knowledge map

Knowledge map is a list of links to dissections. Each link is annotated with a bullet. If you have made enough progress to master the dissection the bullet is a green disk and dissection is availabe to you, otherwise the bullet is a red cross and dissection is unavailable (though you are free to explore it)

The bullt also shows your progress with dissection. As you explore each dissection (below) progress changes from 0%-, to 25%-, 50%-, 75%-, and finally 100%-. The current dissection you are working with is highlighted grey .

Dessections can have prerequisites. If you have made enough progress with all prerequiaites of a dissecion it becomes available. From the very start there is a small number of dissections available (those that do not have prerequisites). As you explore available dissections and make enough progress with them (generally to explore 50% of the dissection is enough progres) more dissections become available.

Menu area

Menu has 3 buttons:

  • - hides(unhides) knowledge map button and gives you more space to work with dissection;
  • - refreshes the knowledge map to update your progress with the dissection;
  • - displays the information you are reading now.

Dissection area

Dissections are displayed in two ways:

  • plain mode (no annotation),
  • advanced more (annotatios available).
You can switch modes by clicking on Code/Dissection links. Boldface indicates current mode.

While in an advanced mode you can click on green bullet next to some lines of code and hence display annotation text, that helps you understand the code better. Each click increments your progress with the dissecion. As you click on more green bullets your progress through dissection. To update your progress click on -reload menu button (above).
